Philosophy first

CUNABULA is designed to help you study philosophy. In turn, philosophy is the systematized study of general and fundamental questions, such as those about existence, reason, knowledge, values, mind, and language. But above all philosophy is the love of wisdom.

Education system

Our goal is to give you a consistent learning, starting with the basics and gradually expanding the basic knowledge. You can choose a direction or study all at once. During the learning process, you will track your progress and receive achievements. The project is completely free. Registration is required to track progress.

History of philosophy

Western philosophy is the philosophical tradition of the Western world, dating back to pre-Socratic thinkers who were active in 6th-century Greece (BCE).

Ontology

Ontology addresses questions like how entities are grouped into categories and which of these entities exist on the most fundamental level.

Epistemology

Epistemologists study the nature, origin, and scope of knowledge, epistemic justification, the rationality of belief, and various related issues.

Duration

Logic

Logic is the study of correct reasoning. It includes both formal and informal logic. Formal logic is the science of deductively valid inferences or of logical truths.
